Goblins are not native to Frostfallen, but they have been known to venture into the kingdom from time to time.
Goblins are a race of small, cunning creatures that are experts at surviving in harsh environments, they are known to be skilled in crafting, often using their abilities to create traps and other devices to defend themselves. 
Goblins are known to be greedy and selfish, often turning on each other in pursuit of power or wealth, they can be unpredictable and dangerous.
